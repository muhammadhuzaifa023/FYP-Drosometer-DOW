Wing Shape



Drosophila flies are a widely used model organism in genetic and developmental studies, and the ability to accurately detect and classify mutations is crucial for further research. With this module, researchers can quickly and easily identify and classify mutations in their flies, streamlining their research process and allowing for more targeted investigations into the underlying genetic and developmental processes.The mutation detection and classification process is powered by a combination of advanced image processing algorithms and machine learning models, ensuring high accuracy and reliability.



This module of the web application is dedicated to detecting and classifying mutations in Drosophila flies. By analyzing images of the fly, the application can accurately determine if the fly is normal or if it exhibits any mutations. If the fly is mutated, the application can further classify the type of mutation that is present.
